Space Facts for Smart Explorers

🌍 Earth

Earth is the only planet we know that has life. It has air, oceans, land, and one natural moon.

🌕 The Moon

The Moon is Earth's natural satellite. NASA's Artemis program is working to send humans back to the Moon and prepare for future Mars missions.

🔴 Mars

Mars is called the Red Planet because iron minerals in its soil make it look reddish.

🛰️ ISS

The International Space Station is a science laboratory in space where astronauts live and do experiments.

🔭 Telescopes

Space telescopes help scientists see faraway stars, galaxies, and planets without Earth's atmosphere blocking the view.

🚀 Rockets

Rockets move upward by pushing hot gas downward. This is called thrust.
