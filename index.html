Space Facts for Smart Kids

🌙 The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. Astronauts first walked on it during Apollo 11 in 1969.

🔴 Mars

Mars is called the Red Planet because iron minerals in its soil make it look rusty red.

🛰️ ISS

The International Space Station is a science laboratory that orbits Earth. Astronauts live and work there.

🚀 Artemis

NASA's Artemis program is working to send humans back to the Moon and prepare for future Mars missions.
